# 🛒 E-Commerce Profitability Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Problem Statement
The company is offering discounts to drive sales across regions and product 
categories, but it's unclear whether this discounting strategy is actually 
profitable. This dashboard analyzes 9,995 e-commerce transactions to identify 
where heavy discounting is hurting profit margins and recommends where the 
company should tighten its discount policy.

## 🔍 Key Insights
- **Furniture** is the least profitable category despite decent sales volume
- **Tables** has the highest loss of all 17 sub-categories
- **Bookcases and Supplies** are also running at a net loss
- Higher discounts directly correlate with negative profit margins
- All 3 customer segments receive similar discount rates (~15%)
- **California and New York** are the biggest sales markets geographically

## 📊 Dashboard Pages
| Page | Title | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Executive Summary | KPI cards + Profit by Category and Sub-Category |
| 2 | Discount Analysis | Scatter plot showing Discount vs Profit relationship |
| 3 | Regional Analysis | Map visual showing Sales and Profit by State |
| 4 | Segment Analysis | Profitability breakdown by Customer Segment |

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop** — Dashboard design and development
- **Power Query** — Data cleaning and transformation
- **DAX** — Custom measures (Profit Margin %, Average Discount, etc.)
- **Dataset** — Sample Superstore (9,995 transactions)

## 📈 DAX Measures Created

- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Average Discount
- Total Orders
